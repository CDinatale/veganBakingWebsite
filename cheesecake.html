Raw Vegan Raspberry Lemon Cheesecake recipe

It may sound complicated, but baking a vegan cheesecake is actually pretty simple! This recipe has been taste-tested and approved by all of my family and friends and I hope you'll enjoy it too! It is the perfect healthy, simple dessert to feed a small crowd (or just you!)
Prep Time Total Time
1 hour 30 min 4 hours

This recipe makes a 6 inch cake.

Ingredients for the crust:

  • 1/4 cup raw almonds
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tbsp flaxseed meal
  • 3 Medjool dates
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil

Ingredients for the lemon layer:

  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 3/4 cup soaked cashews
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup

Ingredients for the rasberry filling:

  • 1/4 cup coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup soaked cashews
  • 1/2 cup raspberries
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions:

1. In a food processor, process the crust ingredients until combined and loose dough forms - it should stick together when you squeeze a bit between your fingers.

2. Add crust to pan lined with parchment paper and press with fingers to distribute. To pack it down, use a small glass wrapped in parchment paper and really press it down. Set in freezer to firm up.

3. Add all lemon filling ingredients to a blender and mix until very smooth. Take pan out of the freezer, spread the mixture evenly over the crust and pop it back in the freezer for about 30 minutes.

4. Add all raspberry filling ingredients to a blender and mix until very smooth. Spread the raspberry mixture over the lemon mixture. Put the pan back in the freezer and let it sit for minimum 4 hours or preferably overnight.

5. When ready to serve, let it thaw for about 5 minutes and enjoy!
